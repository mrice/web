There's more to learning Kotlin than just us -
community tutorials and screencasts

The Kotlin community is big and rapidly getting bigger! Here are some of our favorite tutorials and casts from around the web (organized from easy to hard)

  • Kotlin's Official Tutorials - the folks at JetBrains have wonderful documentation, including their tutorials
  • Tuts Plus - Introduction to Kotlin Programming - this was first published in 2015, and it's a well-known and well-liked overview of Kotlin
  • Kotlin Koans - if you learn best by doing, many in the Kotlin community swear by these exercises (you can even run them in your browser and it works great)
  • Kotlin Uncovered - this is a deep dive on Kotlin by Victoria Gonda. Highly recommended

Just like our own 'casts, we're building this list too. Subscribe to our updates to get more as we find them. Want us to add something? Drop us a note: hello@kotlineschool.com (but keep in mind that we want them to be free, as in beer).
